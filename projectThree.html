Background information

The project: This is a website for Science and Technology company ‘Campsg’ I used to work for. Before this version, the company used a template to contemporarily serve as home pages. The goal of this project was to show company culture and news, in order to win more reliability from both consumers and employees.

1. Focus Group & Interviews

We did a focus group when marketing team communicated with one consumer group. We got CEO, consumers (school leaders) and some employees together to talk about what they want to know or express about the company. During focus group all stakeholders would took notes by category and made audio records at the same time. After that we also had interviews individually with all these stakeholders to get further information and inspirations.

2. analysis result - desires of stakeholdersg

College Leader

potential purchaser

College leaders have duty and obligation to assign educational expenditure.

Job Hunter

potential employee

Job hunters would look company websites as part of important factors.

Student

current user

Students might click links from products they used and see the company websites. Although it seldom happens.

Teacher

current user

Teachers can get in touch directly with salesmen but sometimes they would also look at the company website.

Employee

employees would see company websites as an important corporate image.

3. ideation

Based on 5 key words (reliability, profession, familiarity, sense of belonging and visual look) we collected above, I started ideation to think of feathures that can represent these key words. At the end I concluded 6 main columns: service (product), case, news, techblog, company introduction and recruitment.

4 prototype & visual design

I hope the first page could be a trigger for other parts. For this reason I put the link of company introduction in banner to win attention. Next I listed all products as the biggest part. Followed that were comments and consumer list to show reliability for potential consumers. Just as many websites, I put contact information at the bottom of whole websides.
