Conclusion

Apps in the first category lay an emphasis on creating a gamified and fun experience for users, which is believed to have positive influence on users’ healthy behaviors. However, researchers (Hamari, 2013; Farzan et al. 2008; ) have found that gamification may only create short-term behavioral changes, which has little value to promoting a healthier lifestyle.
